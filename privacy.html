Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

KesariX ("we", "us") runs recruitment campaigns to onboard delivery riders. This policy explains what information we collect when you apply, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

When you submit an application through this site, we collect the details you provide:

  • Your full name
  • Your age
  • Your contact (mobile) number
  • The city you are applying in
  • The hub location you select

We do not ask for or require passwords, bank details or government ID numbers through this form. Additional documents needed for onboarding are collected later, directly by our team.

2. How we use your information

  • To contact you about your application and delivery-partner opportunities
  • To match you with the delivery hub nearest to you
  • To guide you through eligibility checks and onboarding
  • To keep records of our recruitment campaigns

3. How your information is stored

Applications submitted through this site are recorded in a Google Sheet hosted on Google Workspace. Access is limited to authorised KesariX recruitment staff. We retain application data only for as long as needed to process recruitment and follow-up, after which it may be archived or deleted.

4. Third-party services

We rely on a small number of trusted third parties:

  • Google - application data is transmitted and stored via Google Apps Script and Google Sheets.
  • Google AdSense - some pages display advertising served by Google, which may use cookies. See our Cookie Policy.

These providers process data under their own privacy terms.

5. Cookies

We do not set our own tracking cookies. Advertising shown on some pages is served by Google AdSense, which may place cookies to deliver and measure ads. Details and controls are in our Cookie Policy.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the delivery networks and internal teams involved in onboarding you, and where required by law.

7. Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the information you submitted. To make a request, contact us using the details below and we will respond within a reasonable time.
