Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

This policy explains how cookies are used on the KesariX Delivery Club website. We keep our own use of cookies to a minimum - the cookies you may encounter here come from Google advertising.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, to remember preferences, and to measure and serve advertising.

2. Cookies we use

We do not set our own tracking or analytics cookies. The application form works without storing cookies on your device.

Some pages on this site display advertising through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may place advertising cookies on your device to serve ads, limit how often you see a particular ad, and measure ad performance. These cookies are set by Google, not by KesariX.

Note: Advertising cookies are only involved on pages that show ads. If ads are not enabled, no advertising cookies are set.

3. Managing cookies

  • You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.
  • You can manage Google's ad personalisation at myadcenter.google.com.
  • Learn how Google uses cookies in advertising at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Blocking cookies will not stop you from applying - the recruitment form does not depend on them.

4. Changes to this policy

If our use of cookies changes - for example, if we add analytics - we will update this page to reflect that.
