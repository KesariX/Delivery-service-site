About Us

We connect riders with delivery work.

KesariX is a recruitment service. We run onboarding campaigns that help people become delivery partners with last-mile delivery networks across Gujarat.

In short: The KesariX Delivery Club is a recruitment campaign. We collect applications from aspiring delivery riders, verify basic eligibility, and route qualified applicants to the delivery hubs nearest to them.

What we do

We operate recruitment drives for delivery-partner roles. Our job is to make signing up simple - an applicant picks their city and hub, submits a short form, and our team follows up to guide them through onboarding, documentation and activation with the delivery network.

Who we recruit for

We are currently onboarding delivery riders across four Gujarat cities:

  • Surat - Adajan, Vesu, Katargam, Hazira, Ring Road, Old Surat, Magob, Navsari East
  • Vadodara - Chhani, Atladra, Waghodia Road, Shiyabaug Road
  • Bharuch - Zadeshwar, Bholav, Link Road, Old City
  • Ahmedabad - Naroda, CTM, Chandkheda, Makarba, Narol, Navrangpura, Kalol, Gandhinagar, Memco, Kudasan, Sindhubhavan, Chandlodia, Dahegam, Vasna

How it works

  • Apply - fill in your name, age, contact number and choose your nearest hub.
  • We reach out - our team contacts you to confirm details and eligibility.
  • Get onboarded - we guide you through documents, activation and your first deliveries.

Who can apply

Applicants should be between 18 and 55 years of age with a valid contact number. A two-wheeler, a driving licence and standard identity documents are typically required to be activated as a delivery partner, and are confirmed during onboarding.
